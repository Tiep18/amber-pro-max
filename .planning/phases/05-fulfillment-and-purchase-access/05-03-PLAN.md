**As a** paid customer, **I want to** reopen prior purchases from my account or claim a guest order, **so that** my PDF and tracking access stays durable without leaking another customer's data.

</objective>

<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| signed-in account -> order/library projections | Authenticated customer reads sensitive order, payment, download, and tracking summaries. |
| guest browser -> reopen/claim actions | Untrusted order/email/token inputs attempt to reopen or claim prior purchases. |
| claim transaction -> ownership boundary | A guest order becomes account-owned and old guest tokens must stop working. |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-05-03-01 | Elevation of Privilege | account order/library projections | mitigate | Owner predicates, `security_invoker = true` views where used, RLS tests, and no `TO authenticated`-only policies. |
| T-05-03-02 | Information Disclosure | guest reopen and claim pages | mitigate | Generic success/error copy, no order/email/PDF existence confirmation, and no internal ID/token rendering. |
| T-05-03-03 | Spoofing | order claim tokens | mitigate | Hash tokens at rest, 24-hour expiry, same-email signed-in check, consume-on-use status, and replay tests. |
| T-05-03-04 | Tampering | claim ownership update | mitigate | Single transaction updates owner, revokes guest tokens, and writes audit with safe metadata. |
| T-05-03-SC | Tampering | package installs | accept | No package installs in this plan. |
</threat_model>
